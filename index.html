Welcome to my Game of Life !

In this game, you draw cells on the board and hit the play button, and/or set the speed at which the game will play.

Rules are simple :

  • Any live cell with two or three live neighbours survives.
  • Any dead cell with three live neighbours becomes a live cell.
  • All other live cells die in the next generation. Similarly, all other dead cells stay dead.

Things that can emerge from this simple steps can be just amazing. Try it !
